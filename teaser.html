HomeCaptain  ·  a Forever Apps project

The complete information layer for your home.

Your house generates records for decades — repairs, warranties, bills, blueprints, the plumber's number. All of it scatters across drawers, inboxes, and memory. HomeCaptain is the one place that holds it, understands it, and hands it back the moment you need it.

01

The problem nobody has solved

A home is the largest asset most people ever own, and the worst-documented. When the water heater fails, you can't remember who installed it or whether it's under warranty. When you file an insurance claim, you're reconstructing a lifetime of contents from nothing. When you sell, everything you learned about the house walks out the door with you.

There is no source of truth for a home. HomeCaptain is that source of truth — and the assistant that reads it back to you.

02

What it holds

Memory

The Logbook

Every repair, upgrade, and service visit — cost, vendor, photos, notes. Searchable history and spend by category. It only gets more valuable the longer you own the home.

Vault

Documents & Blueprints

Bills, warranties, policies, permits, closing docs — and the floorplans and survey. Scanned, tied to the home, and instantly findable.

Answers

Ask Your Manuals

Snap a model number and the manual is captured for good — then just ask it. "How do I reset the dishwasher?" is answered from the actual document, not a drawer you'll dig through and lose again.

Protection

Insurance Inventory

Room-by-room contents with photos and values, one tap from a claim-ready PDF. Photograph a room and the AI drafts the list for you. Most people have nothing when disaster strikes.

Foresight

Maintenance Engine

Recurring and seasonal reminders — filters, gutters, HVAC, winterizing — tailored to the home's age, systems, and climate. And because it knows your models, "filter's due" comes with the exact part, one tap from reordered.

Trust

Find a Pro & Quotes

Search vetted contractors near the home, keep the ones you've hired with your own rating, and let the AI read a repair quote and tell you what to ask before you sign.

Intelligence

AI Concierge

Scan a bill into a record. Summarize a home's history and spend. "What's due?" "Where's the water shutoff?" The connective tissue across everything above.

Design

Reimagine the Space

HomeCaptain isn't only about what breaks. Explore a redesign — furniture, paint, and style to try before you buy — on a to-scale floorplan that tells you whether the sofa actually fits before you order it.

Why now

Homeowners have never had more to track and fewer tools to track it with. Insurers want documented inventories. Property records, warranties, and smart-home gear multiply every year. Yet the category has no owner — no product that treats the home as a living, queryable record rather than a folder of PDFs.

The building blocks finally exist to do it cheaply: a small, modern stack and AI that can read a bill, grade a quote, or draft an inventory from a photo. The window is open, and the seat is empty.

Where it's headed

The foundation is poured. The interesting work is ahead.

HomeCaptain starts as the place your home's records finally live — and grows into the assistant that reads them back, protects what matters, and helps you make the place better. One home at a time.
